The scatter plot depicts the speed of CMEs over time. The horizontal axis displays the chronological sequence, with each data point placed along the timeline according to its occurrence. On the other hand, the vertical axis indicates the velocity of the CMEs in kilometers per second. Every dot on the graph is represented by a circle, positioned according to the specific time and speed of a CME. These circles all have the same size, determined by the `circleRadius` variable. By capturing the relevant information from NASA's API, running it through some processing, and finally displaying it in an SVG element on the webpage, the code allows us to easily perceive the fluctuations in CME speeds over the defined time frame.
